# 🏠 3D Interior Design Studio

A fully interactive, real-time 3D interior design tool built with Three.js. Design your dream room by adjusting dimensions, placing furniture, changing colors, and exporting your creation—all in your browser.

## 🚀 Live Demo
[Click here to try it live!](https://your-username.github.io/interior-studio)

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Room Customization
- **Adjust dimensions** – Width, Depth, and Height sliders update the room in real-time.
- **Wall Colors** – Choose from 8 curated swatches or pick a custom color.
- **Floor Textures** – 8 different wood and tile styles.

### 🎮 Interactions
- **Click to Place** – Select a furniture type, then click the floor to place it.
- **Drag to Move** – Click and drag any placed item to reposition it.
- **Rotate** – Select an item and press `R` or click the Rotate button.
- **Delete** – Right-click an item, press `Delete`, or use the Delete button.
- **Snap to Grid** – Toggle snapping for precise alignment (0.5m grid).
- **Grid Overlay** – Show/hide the floor grid for reference.

### 🔧 Pro Tools
- **Undo/Redo** – Full history with `Ctrl+Z` / `Ctrl+Y` or toolbar buttons.
- **Export PNG** – Capture a high-resolution screenshot of your design.
- **Save/Load** – Export your room as a `.json` file and reload it anytime.
- **Dark/Light Theme** – Toggle between dark and light UI modes.

### 📊 Performance
- **Real-time FPS counter** – Monitor performance.
- **Optimized rendering** – Shadows and lighting without sacrificing frame rate.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JS** – No frameworks, pure frontend.
- **Three.js (r128)** – 3D rendering engine.
- **OrbitControls** – Camera controls (rotate, pan, zoom).
- **LocalStorage** – Theme persistence.
